Why it makes sense

Glasses and contact lenses are an expensive habit

Contact lenses, cleaning solutions, frames, optician visits — year after year, the costs add up to a significant sum. Flow3 is a one-off investment that pays for itself in just 4–5 years.

Contact lenses: 280–720 €/year

Monthly lenses, cleaning products and regular check-ups — the average wearer spends 3 000–7 000 euros over 10 years.

Glasses: 150–400 €/year

New lenses every 2 years, frame upgrades, separate glasses for sun and work.

Daily time cost

Inserting lenses, maintenance, fiddling with glasses — it adds up to over 100 hours per year.

Health risks

Swimming with contact lenses increases the risk of Acanthamoeba keratitis by up to 50 times. After Flow3, you swim freely.

Up to 7 years warranty
